We exist to serve every patient, every hour.

HealthMed Hospital is a fully registered medical facility located at 9 Etim Okon Usanga Close, Off Nwaniba by Water Fountain, Uyo, Akwa Ibom State. We are committed to providing accessible, quality healthcare to individuals and families in our community — 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Our hospital offers a comprehensive range of services including emergency care, family medicine, maternity services, child care, general surgery, laboratory diagnostics, pharmacy, and mental health support. Every service is available around the clock because we understand that medical needs do not follow a schedule.

We believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to everyone. Our team of qualified and experienced medical professionals — doctors, nurses, laboratory scientists, and support staff — works tirelessly to ensure that every patient who walks through our doors receives the highest standard of care delivered with compassion and dignity.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Compassion

We treat every patient with empathy, respect, and dignity — understanding that illness affects more than just the body.

Quality

We are committed to delivering the highest standard of medical care through qualified staff, accurate diagnostics, and evidence-based treatment.

Availability

Medical emergencies don't keep office hours. We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Community

We are deeply rooted in Uyo and Akwa Ibom. Serving our community is not just our business — it is our calling.

Our Infrastructure

Built to care without limits.

HealthMed Hospital is equipped with reliable infrastructure that ensures uninterrupted service — no matter the conditions outside.

Uninterrupted Power Supply

Our hospital runs on a dual power system — backup generator + solar panels — ensuring every department stays lit and every machine stays on, even during grid failures.

Functioning Water Supply

Clean, running water is available throughout the hospital — from wards to theatres to the laboratory — maintaining the hygiene standards essential for patient safety.

Onsite Laboratory

Our fully equipped on-site laboratory handles a wide range of diagnostic tests with same-day results. No need to refer patients elsewhere — everything is done under one roof.
