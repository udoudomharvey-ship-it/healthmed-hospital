How It Works

From test request to results — fast.

1
Doctor's Request

Your doctor at HealthMed issues a test request form after your consultation.

2
Sample Collection

Blood, urine, or stool samples are collected at our dedicated sample point by trained staff.

3
Lab Processing

Samples are processed immediately by our onsite laboratory scientists using modern equipment.

4
Same-Day Results

Most results are ready the same day. Your doctor reviews and discusses them with you immediately.
