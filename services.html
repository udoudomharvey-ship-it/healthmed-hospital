Infrastructure

Systems that keep us running.

Behind every service is a reliable infrastructure that ensures nothing stops your care.

Uninterrupted Power Supply

Backup generator + solar power system ensures zero downtime. All critical equipment stays on even during grid outages — every service, always running.

Functioning Water Supply

Clean, running water available across all wards, theatres, and departments — essential for hygiene, sterilisation, and patient care.

Onsite Laboratory

Fully equipped diagnostic lab on the premises. Same-day results for blood tests, microscopy, and more — no external referrals needed.
